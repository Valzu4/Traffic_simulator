\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{structCityStats}{City\+Stats}} class is used to store the city as a grid of squares. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classCity}{City}} is a grid of squares. \mbox{\hyperlink{classCity}{City}} also tracs many statistics, e.\+g. time, and the ammount of total traffic in the city.
