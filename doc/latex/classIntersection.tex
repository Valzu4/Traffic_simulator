\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classIntersection}{Intersection}} represents an intersection in the traffic simulation. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classIntersection}{Intersection}} contains output and input spots for cars and pedestrians and simulates them according to the traffic laws. They move the cars and pedestrians where they need to go.
