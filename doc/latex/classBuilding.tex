\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} represents an building in the traffic simulation. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} contains a vector for residents. When the resident is about to leave, it is moved to either pedestrians vector, or if going by car, it is moved to a car and cars have their own vector. There are also different type of buildings and different type of buildings have different capacities for residents.
